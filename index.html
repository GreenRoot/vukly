About Vukly

Vukly is not just another fitness app - it's a revolutionary platform designed specifically for the Telegram mini app ecosystem. We're bringing the power of personalized fitness, nutrition guidance, and community support right to your favorite messaging app.

As participants in the Telegram mini app accelerator, we're at the forefront of integrating cutting-edge technology with everyday communication tools. Our mission is to make fitness accessible, engaging, and seamlessly integrated into your digital life.
